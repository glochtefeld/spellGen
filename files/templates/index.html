Spell Press

Quickly and easily generate randomly prepared spells for your Dungeons and Dragons Character (5th Edition). If you need spells for a character as soon as possible and don't intend to play it for very long, this is an excellent way to prompt creative solutions with whatever spells you are dealt.

Example Spellbooks

View a few spellbooks for characters at a variety of levels.

Generator

This is where spellbooks are generated. Simply put in your class, level, spellcasting modifier, and whether or not you wish to include spells from multiple rule books.

Dice Roller

This is a separate project of mine, to simplify some of the more complicated rolls a player might need to make. Ultimately, it is unrelated to this project, but still interesting.
